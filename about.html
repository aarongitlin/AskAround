So, what's Ask Around the World all about?

Meaningful dialogue is the key to mutual understanding and a better world for all.

What is this?

Ask Around the World gives you the opportunity to ask the question that's most important to you in the world and connects you with good people across our planet.

We encourage you to upload/record your question and to share it with everyone you know. For every question we publish, we will donate US$1 to a worldwide charitable cause.

Ask Around the World is an AskVote.com initiative.

Be one of the first people in the world to join the conversation revolution, just subscribe at the bottom of the page.

Why did we create Ask Around the World?

In the words of explorer Wade Davis: "to find those great stories of wonder found within various cultures that teach us something about human experience."

It's important to question everything, including ourselves, right? With that in mind, we figure it's a good idea to begin with the most important questions first. We also believe that meaningful dialogue is the key to mutual understanding and a better world for all. It's simple, really.

How does this work?

Using Ask Around the World is easy. All you need is a YouTube or Google account. You can upload your video question from your YouTube account or you can record directly using your webcam.

  • Upload/Record: Ask the question that's most important to you in the world. Make it short (10 seconds). Make it meaningful.
  • Share: Post it, tweet it, shout it from your window.
  • Watch: Look at what other people are asking.
  • High-five: You've just done humankind a favour. Dance around the room.
