UPLOAD/RECORD

It’s question time! You’re about the upload a question to Ask Around the World

Uploading/recording a question works best if you’re using the Chrome browser, but should also work using Firefox, Safari and the latest version of Explorer.

You will need a Google/YouTube account to ask your question. Most Google accounts are automatically linked with YouTube, but some older or business accounts may require authorisation at youtube.com.

You can choose to upload a file from your computer/hard-drive/camera etc. Or you can record directly through your webcam.

If you're having trouble with the uploader, you can also post a question as a video response to the Ask Around the World YouTube channel.

The webcam recorder will function best with up to date versions of Adobe Flash and your webcam's firmware.

To record directly from your webcam you need to give permission for YouTube to access/manage your account.

Once you’ve uploaded or recorded your question, it might take some time (up to 24 hours) for Ask Around the World to review and post your question on the site.

Why submit? Well, it's for a good cause. For every video we publish, Ask Around the World will donate US$1 to a range of global aid organisations. We’ll also choose our favourite 10 submissions and donate a further US$200 on behalf of each.
